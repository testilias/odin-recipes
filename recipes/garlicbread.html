Garlic Bread

Description

Ingredients:

  • Bread of your preferrence
  • Butter
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Herbs&Spices

Preparation:

  1. Slice your bread lengthwise and put it on baking sheet
  2. Mix the garlic with the spices and butter, then spread on bread
  3. Bake for 10-15 mins at 200 Celsius
  4. Gratz!!!!
